ABOUT ME

My interest in working as a UI/UX designer stems from my desire to combine creativity with functionality in the process of creating web and mobile applications. I aim to design user interfaces that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also provide intuitive usage and a pleasant user experience. Through this professional path, I aspire to learn and apply best design practices, understand user needs, and create products that meet their expectations and deliver value. I believe that working in the field of UI/UX design is an exciting challenge that allows me to develop my design skills and collaborate with different teams to achieve the common goal of creating high-quality and usable products.
