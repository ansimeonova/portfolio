ABOUT ME

I am a passionate UI/UX designer dedicated to crafting intuitive and user-centered experiences. During my internship at Figmaister, I gained hands-on experience in user research, wireframing, and prototyping, refining my ability to translate complex ideas into seamless designs. Currently, I independently manage two freelance projects. One involves direct collaboration with a lead developer to ensure smooth design-to-development handoff, while in the other, I work closely with the client, guiding them through the design process to bring their vision to life. Through these projects, I’ve strengthened my skills in design systems, accessibility, and iterative problem-solving. I thrive at the intersection of creativity and functionality, always eager to explore new tools, optimize workflows, and craft impactful digital experiences.
